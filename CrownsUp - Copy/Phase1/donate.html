Your giving holds a Crown up! Give today!

We invite you today to have a direct impact on young queens’ lives by making a donation. Your gift ensures that Crowns Up! can continue to inspire girls to dream beyond what is possible and to empower them to make their dreams a reality. Majority of our programs are at no cost to the families we serve. The funds we raise allow us to keep our services affordable to the communities that need us most.

$25 will provide transportation to and from sessions for one month.

$50 will provide a young queen with snacks for one month.

$100 will provide after school programming supplies for one month.

$250 will provide a young queen with a summer camp experience where she will be educated, exposed to new things, and empowered to change the world.

$300 will provide items for our store that aids in teaching girls financial literacy.

$500 will provide a young queen with the opportunity to attend the Educate Me College Tour.
The Educate ME College Tour provides cultural and educational experiences that inspire students to examine concerns and personal interest during the college selection process. Students have a chance to walk through academic, residential, athletic and administrative buildings, but also meet with faculty, financial aid counselors, and admissions representatives to learn more about each school’s admissions policies and deadlines.

$750 will provide a cohort a young queens with a field trip where they can experience hands on learning.

$800 will provide an entire summer camp with enhancement partners that will teach young queens how to express themselves using art and technology.

$1500 will provide an entire cohort with a team building retreat. The retreats are used to develop more productive relationships and improve levels of communication and cooperation amongst the group. Retreats also improve the teams’ weaknesses and build on their strengths
