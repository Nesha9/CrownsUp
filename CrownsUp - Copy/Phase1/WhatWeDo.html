Programs


After School Programing

Crowns Up! meets once a week after school at select schools within Davidson County. Young queens will learn the necessary knowledge, skills, and attitudes needed to succeed in life and in school. Crowns Up! strives to create a warm and fun environment where young queens participate in activities which engage girls in topics such as communication, stress management, media literacy, college/career readiness, financial literacy, body image and confidence building. Crowns Up! Mentoring

Mentors are one of the many critical resources in facilitating the social emotional, cognitive, and behavioral development of a youth by continuously and consistently providing support and guidance.
